Project Overview

Built to solve a real-world problem I observed as a business analyst: teams reusing templates without updating critical fields, leading to incorrect reports and unnecessary rework.

The Problem

Business teams frequently reuse last year's files but forget to update:

  • Fiscal years and quarters (2024 ‚Üí 2025)
  • Date references (Q1 2024, Jan 2024)
  • Column headers and report titles
  • Team member names and roles
  • Version numbers and copyright dates

Result: Incorrect data, embarrassing errors in client presentations, and hours wasted on manual fixes.

‚úÖ

The Solution

FileRefresh automatically scans uploaded files and:

  • Detects 10+ types of stale data patterns
  • Shows side-by-side before/after previews
  • Allows users to review and edit suggestions
  • Outputs updated files in original format
  • Works entirely in-browser (no uploads to servers)

Impact: Reduces template update time from 30+ minutes to under 2 minutes while eliminating human error.

How It Works

Simple 4-step process from upload to download

1

Upload Your File

Drag and drop or browse for any Excel, PowerPoint, Word, or PDF file. Set the year range you want to update (e.g., 2024 ‚Üí 2025).

2

Scan for Stale Data

The tool reads your file and identifies all instances of old years, dates, labels, version numbers, and team names that may need updating.

3

Review & Confirm Changes

See every proposed change with old ‚Üí new previews. Check/uncheck items, edit suggestions, and confirm only what you want to update.

4

Download Updated File

Get your file back in the same format, ready to use. All changes applied, zero manual errors.
